PROJECT VFN (#ViolencefreeNg)
Adenike with her team of 9 worked with LEAP Africa for the Youth Day of Service Program (YDOS) with the theme “Youthful and Useful” to commemorate International Youth Day 2021. She led a team of 9 young people to organize and implemented a project titled “Violence Free Nigeria” (Project VFN) for 114 beneficiaries in 2 schools. Sensitization to addressed Sexual and Gender-Based Violence (SGBV) was done for young people to increase awareness on how to prevent and protect themselves and others from all forms of gender-based violence, abuse and exploitation, and ensure that young people who has experienced or experiencing violence receive prompt services and access to justice.

I am open to working with a great teams on a project that interest me and solves both developers and users problems. I constantly keep my skillset up by teaching students and reading books spanning client side programming, server side programming and automation.
